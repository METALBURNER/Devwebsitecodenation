1.1 Describing the purpose of IT in modern business;

Starting in the early 1980s with the first desktop computers, information technology has played an important part in global economies. Companies rely on IT for fast communications, data processing and market intelligence. IT plays an integral role in every industry, helping companies improve business processes, achieve cost efficiencies, drive revenue growth and maintain a competitive advantage in the marketplace. Also with the global growth in recognition about watching how your business is conducted, making sure you are seen as a “green” environmentally friendly. IT allows you to do this by being as paperless as possible by using E-Mail and other IT systems that are readily available.
